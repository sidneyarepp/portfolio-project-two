Intro

Hello! My name is Sidney Repp and I am an aspiring fullstack web developer based out of Detroit, Michigan. I love being challenged with difficult tasks that require a lot of problem solving. Learning to code over the past year has provided that challenge I enjoy, and I hope to get the opportunity to work alongside talented developers that I can learn from while I grow as a developer.

By the time I complete my course I hope to be fully able to provide a company with fullstack skills that include HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, as well as several libraries to successfully create interactive apps. I currently have a background in E-commerce with experience in the following:

  • Python
  • Microsoft Office: Excel, Word, PowerPoint, and Outlook
  • Adobe Creative Suite: Photoshop, Illustrator
  • Photography

Thank you for looking at my site, and if you'd like to contact me please reach out to me through email, LinkedIn, or GitHub.
